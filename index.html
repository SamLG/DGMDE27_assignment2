Browse this site for some useful tips and tricks

  • Diet & Nutrition

    We all know that this is perhaps the best change we can make to lose weight and get healthy. We all need to eat more vegetables, consume more fruit, decrease our consumtion of fatty processed foods, ...etc.

    Little changes everyday can make big changes overall. We need to ask ourselves some important questions. Are we really hungry? Is this really worth it? (those with eating disorders should disregard the prior questions)

  • Exercise

    This is so crucial to being healthy. We need activity for endorphins and overall quality of life. Get outside and get moving. Get a little vitamin D for happiness and let your body be strong.

    Do you remember when you were little and you could do anything? When we are little we exercise all the time, but we seem to forget how important and fun movement and exertion is. Don't forget.

  • Mental Power

    It's all well and good to say that diet and nutrition are the basis of good health and weight, but we all know this and yet most of us struggle. The real power is in our mentalities.

    We have to commit to making healthy choices and lifelong changes. Fad diets and exercise every blue moon will not give us the long term benefits that real life changes can create.
