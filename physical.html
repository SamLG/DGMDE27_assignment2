Go For A Walk

This is one of the simplest and easiest ways to get more active. And there is also the added benefit of the mental relaxation of the rhthymic movement. We were originally hunters and gatherers and walking is such a natural state for us to be in. It if very unhealthy how much we sit each day, so give your self the gift of a walk.

Find Your Fun

In our society (North America) habitually reward ourselves with food for a job well done. We need to get into the mindset of thinking of food as sustenance and rewarding ourselves in other ways.

I suggest we find fun activities like kayaking, rock climbing, swimming, dancing, to reward ourselves instead. Exercise should make you feel good and treating yourself to an activity that gets you moving and is fun is so much better than reaching for a slice of cake.
